📈 The Problem

  • Defence & emergency zones lack compact, smart, GPS-free surveillance
  • Existing drones are bulky, GPS-dependent, expensive, and unsuitable for indoor use

🚀 The Solution

SSSS Drone (Small Sized Smart Surveillance) - GPS-independent, lightweight, low-cost, real-time monitoring with indoor tactical capability.

⚙️ Key Features

  • Power: 3.7V Li-ion Battery
  • Control: Remote Controlled
  • Navigation: No GPS Required
  • Payload: Camera for live surveillance
  • Assembly: Manual via RC Drone Kit
  • Mission Planning Tool: Integrated

🌍 Real-Time Use Cases

  • 🏢 Urban Military Recon (Indoor hostile zones)
  • ⚡ Disaster Monitoring (Collapsed structures)
  • 🏣 Railway/Metro Surveillance
  • 🚓 Traffic & Civilian Monitoring
  • 🏫 Training Kits for Institutions

₹8000+ Cr tactical drone market in India over next 5 years. Huge push from Make in India. Rising need in urban and border surveillance.

💸 Revenue Model
